👨‍💻 About Me

Hi, I'm Shruti Joshi, a Research Scholar pursuing my PhD in Neuroscience at the University of California San Diego in the Bazhenov Lab. My research focuses on understanding olfactory processing in the brain and exploring the intersection of neuroscience and machine learning.

I'm particularly interested in how insights from neuroscience can inform machine learning algorithms and how computational approaches can advance our understanding of neural processes.
